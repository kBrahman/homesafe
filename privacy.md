# Privacy Policy for Homesafe Security Hub

**Effective Date:** January 3, 2026

Homesafe Security Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Telegram bot and related services handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection
We process video streams solely for the purpose of transmitting them between your designated devices. We do not store video content on our servers.

## 2. Permissions
The app requires Camera and Microphone access to function as a security monitor. This data is never shared with third parties.

## 3. Contact Us
If you have questions, please contact support via the Telegram Bot command /help.
